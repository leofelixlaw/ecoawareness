Faq

Requently ask Questions & Answers

  • 01 How often do I need to refill the disinfectant solutions?

    On average the disinfectant (500 ml pack) is refilled every 6 months based on 8 hours of continuous usage per day , or when the transparent refill bottle at the back of unit shown it is empty.

  • 02 Once switch on, how long does it take for the humidity airflow to cover an area of 3,000 sq ft?

    Usually it will take up to 1 hour to fill up 3000 square feet (by 10 feet ceiling height) area. However the time may vary somewhat depending on is the space enclosed, open or is there other external source of airflows.

  • 03 How long is the effectiveness of disinfectant solutions on various surfaces (eg: wall, furniture, etc)?

    When using EcondLIFE with the INCU ++29 nano copper disinfectant , the disinfectant particles which in contact with walls and furniture within the recommended space can expect to last for up to 24 hours.

  • 04 Any evidence to show that Nano Copper solution kill viruses?

    Yes, there are plenty of proven research reports internationally by accredited specialists and reputable organizations showing effectiveness of Nano copper disinfecting numerous bacteria and viruses. NSF / ANSI / CAN60.

  • 05 How much water is needed to operate Econdlife and how often to refill water?

    Econdlife water tank has a 16-litre capacity and in a normal room temperature environment can operate up to 10 hours. We recommend to refill water when the water tank is showing level is low.

  • 06 What is the electricity consumption of EcondLIFE?

    The power rating is 130W, or 0.56 Ampere per hour, about RM 0.03 or 3 cents per hour based on residential home usage.

  • 07 What is the product warranty period?

    One (1) year, with extended warranty which can be purchased.

  • 08 How is the maintenance and service centre?

    With daily use it is recommended to service it every 6 months. Our service centres provide 7-day service support on appointment basis.

  • 09 Does the disinfectant use produce side effects on pregnancy, young children, elderly and those suffering from Asthma conditions?

    Econdlife’s INCU ++29 disinfectant is very safe to use with 100% organic content and approved by NSF and ANSI in USA for human safety and standards. The refilling of the disinfectant container and handling of the machine should be done by adult and children are refrain to perform this task. And ONLY our INCU ++29 disinfectant to be used and we don’t recommend any other liquid or disinfectant as it will affect the disinfecting performance this device.

  • 10 Any other health precaution related to use of Nano Copper solutions?

    This Nano copper solution is strictly for disinfectant usage and added to water only for achieving optimal performance. It is a commonsense that in its concentrated form it is not meant for “drinking” or direct contact with the body or face or eyes.
